Chris's Story

Meet Chris Smith.

On November 24, 2021, Chris’s life was forever changed when he was shot in the head and left for dead after going on a first date with a woman whose ex-boyfriend carried out the attack.

Against overwhelming odds, Chris survived. A 9mm hollow-point bullet remains lodged in Chris’s frontal lobe.

This horrific act became the catalyst for an extraordinary journey of resilience, determination, and recovery. Through immense physical, emotional, and mental challenges, Chris rebuilt his life and discovered a powerful purpose: to help others find strength, hope, and perspective through adversity.

Today, Christopher J. Smith is a motivational and inspirational speaker who shares his story to remind audiences that even in the darkest moments, the human spirit can rise, heal, and thrive.
