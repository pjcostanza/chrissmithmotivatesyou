From Zero Percent to Purpose

Christopher J. Smith’s life changed forever on November 24, 2021, when he was shot point‑blank in the head and left for dead after going on a first date. A 9mm hollow‑point bullet remains lodged in his frontal lobe.

Doctors informed his parents that he had a zero percent chance of survival or recovery. Christopher suffered three major ischemic strokes, spent more than two months in a coma, and was told he would never walk, talk, or live independently again.

Against every medical prediction, he survived—and recovered.

Christopher’s journey from the ICU to full independence became the foundation of his mission: Christopher J. Smith Motivational & Inspirational Speaking.

Today, Christopher is an international bestselling author of My Fatal First Date, has appeared on CBS’s 48 Hours and Fox & Friends, filmed a Roku Network documentary, served as a keynote speaker for the Brain Injury Association of America (BIAA), and currently has a feature film script in development based on his story.

His message is clear: the power of the human spirit is unstoppable—and that power exists within us all.

Speaking Audiences

Who Christopher Speaks To

Hospitals & Rehabilitation Facilities

Christopher speaks to inpatient and outpatient rehabilitation facilities, helping patients and families believe that healing is possible—even after catastrophic injury. As a documented zero‑percent survivor, he offers real‑world proof that recovery can happen.

Businesses & Organizations

Business owners and HR leaders invite Christopher to motivate teams, increase resilience, and unlock untapped potential. His message strengthens mindset, accountability, and performance both professionally and personally.

Sports Teams

As a former college football player, Christopher works with athletic directors, coaches, and teams at the high school, college, and professional levels to build mental toughness, discipline, and championship culture.

Churches & Faith Communities

Christopher delivers a faith‑centered testimony on the power of prayer and God’s presence in life’s darkest moments, strengthening belief and affirming that miracles still happen through Jesus Christ.

Schools & Universities

Christopher motivates students to make strong choices, avoid common pitfalls, and live with purpose and discipline—teaching that decisions made early in life shape long‑term outcomes.
