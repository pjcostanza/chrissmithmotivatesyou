About Christopher J. Smith

Motivational & Inspirational Speaker • Survivor • Author • Faith‑Driven

From Zero Percent to Purpose

Hello, my name is Chris Smith. On November 24, 2021, I was shot point‑blank in the head and left for dead after going on a first date. A 9mm hollow‑point bullet remains lodged in my frontal lobe.

Doctors told my parents I had a zero percent chance of survival or recovery. I suffered three major ischemic strokes, spent more than two months in a coma, and was told I would never walk, talk, or live independently again.

They were wrong.

I am living proof that recovery is possible against all odds. My journey from the ICU to full life is what fuels my mission: Christopher J. Smith Motivational & Inspirational Speaking.

Today, I am an international bestselling author of My Fatal First Date, have appeared on CBS’s 48 Hours, Fox & Friends, filmed a Roku Network documentary, served as a keynote speaker for the Brain Injury Association of America (BIAA), and now a feature film script is being written based on my story.

The power of the human spirit is unstoppable—and I teach audiences how to unlock that same power within themselves.

Who I Speak To

Hospitals & Rehabilitation Facilities

I speak to inpatient and outpatient rehabilitation facilities, helping patients and families see that healing is possible—even after catastrophic injury. As a zero‑percenter myself, I show patients that recovery can happen when hope feels lost.

Businesses & Organizations

Business owners and HR leaders bring me in to motivate teams, increase resilience, and unlock untapped potential. I help employees improve performance, mindset, and personal accountability—at work and in life.

Sports Teams

As a former college football player, I work with athletic directors, coaches, and teams at the high school, college, and professional levels. I instill a championship mindset, mental toughness, and a never‑quit attitude.

Churches & Faith Communities

I share a faith‑based message on the power of prayer and God’s presence during life’s darkest moments. My testimony strengthens belief and affirms that miracles still happen through Jesus Christ.

Schools & Universities

I motivate students to make strong choices, avoid common pitfalls, and live life with purpose and discipline. I teach that decisions made at a young age shape the rest of our lives.
